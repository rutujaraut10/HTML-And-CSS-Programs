WELCOME TO A. C. PATIL COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING

A.C. Patil College of Engineering (ACPCE), located in Kharghar, Navi Mumbai, is a renowned institution affiliated with the University of Mumbai and approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE). The college offers a range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering, including Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.) degrees in disciplines such as Computer Engineering, Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, and Information Technology. Known for its quality education and state-of-the-art facilities, ACPCE focuses on fostering technical skills and innovation among its students, preparing them for successful careers in the engineering industry.

In addition to academic programs, ACPCE encourages students to participate in extracurricular activities and technical clubs, promoting overall personality development and innovation. The college regularly hosts seminars, conferences, and technical competitions, providing students with platforms to showcase their skills and stay updated with the latest advancements in technology. ACPCE's strong industry connections facilitate excellent placement opportunities for its graduates, with many students securing positions in top national and international companies. With its commitment to fostering technical expertise and innovation, A.C. Patil College of Engineering continues to be a preferred choice for aspiring engineers.
